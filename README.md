# 📊 E-Commerce Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an end-to-end E-commerce Performance Dashboard built using Power BI. It analyzes user traffic, conversion funnel, product performance, and user behavior to uncover actionable business insights.

The dataset includes ~472K sessions, ~32K orders, and ~$1.94M in total revenue.

---

## 🎯 Key Insights

* 📈 Sessions and revenue show consistent growth, reaching ~145K monthly sessions at peak
* 📉 A major drop-off (~64%) occurs at the Product → Cart stage, indicating weak purchase intent
* 🏆 Revenue is heavily concentrated in a few top-performing products
* 💸 High refund rates in key products contribute significantly to revenue loss
* 🔁 Repeat users (~17% of users) generate higher conversion rates (~7.8%) than new users (~6.6%)
* 💻 Desktop conversion (~8.5%) is significantly higher than mobile (~3.1%), highlighting UX issues

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Overview

* KPIs: ~472K Sessions | ~32K Orders | ~6.8% Conversion Rate | ~$1.94M Revenue
* Monthly trends show strong growth with seasonal peaks
* Traffic dominated by non-brand search, indicating strong acquisition but low brand recognition

---

### 2️⃣ Funnel Analysis

* Significant drop at Product → Cart (~64%)
* Users reaching billing stage show high purchase intent
* Opportunity to improve product page engagement and checkout flow

---

### 3️⃣ Product Performance

* Top product drives the majority of revenue
* Same product also contributes to the highest refund-related revenue loss
* Indicates dependency risk and need for quality improvement

---

### 4️⃣ User Behavior

* New users contribute ~83% of traffic but have lower conversion rates
* Repeat users convert better, indicating higher intent
* Desktop users outperform mobile significantly
* Average engagement: ~2.5 pages/session and ~1.24 items/order
